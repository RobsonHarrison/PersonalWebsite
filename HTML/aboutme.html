ALL ABOUT ME.

Where do I begin? My name is Robson, although I'm sure the header gives that away! I'm 25 years old and live in the seaside town of Scarborough with my partner, Georgia.

I have always lived in Scarborough. It is famous for its vibrant seafront with arcades that attract hundreds of thousands of tourists a year.

From a young age I held a keen interest in computing and technology. Completing my GCSE studies early, I spent the remainder of my secondary school IT lessons helping the school's IT Technician.

When you strive to become better than we are, everything around you becomes better too.
